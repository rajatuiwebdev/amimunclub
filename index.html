"PAX PER PRAXIS" : Peace Through Action

CONFERENCE BEGINS IN

Welcome to AMIMUN' 24!

I extend to you all a heartfelt welcome to the prestigious 13th edition of the Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN'24). It is with great pleasure that I, Gauri Sareen, the Secretary-General of AMIMUN’24, invite you to join us at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh, for an enriching and enlightening experience from Friday, January 19th, to Sunday, January 21st, 2024. AMIMUN has consistently evolved over the years, becoming a beacon of excellence within the Indian MUN circuit and attracting a diverse and accomplished international delegation. Our commitment to fostering meaningful dialogue and promoting innovative solutions to global challenges remains steadfast. In pursuit of these objectives, the theme for AMIMUN'24 theme is "Pax Per Praxis," which translates to "Peace Through Action." Our theme underscores the significance of action, of turning noble words into impactful deeds. It beckons us to explore the realms of hope, resilience, and shared sovereignty. It is our belief that true change is born from audacious decisions and unwavering commitment. We firmly believe that actions speak louder than words, and it is through our collective efforts that we can bring about the positive change the world needs. This conference offers you not only a platform to showcase your debating and diplomatic skills but also an opportunity to inspire change and champion resilience. Our team is dedicated to ensure that your AMIMUN'24 experience transcends the committee rooms. We aim to provide an environment that stimulates personal growth and international collaboration, preparing you to become leaders in an ever-changing world. It is our hope that you will leave this conference with a renewed sense of purpose and a commitment to creating a better future. I would like to express my sincere gratitude to our Founder President Sir, Chancellor Sir, our guiding light Vice Chancellor Ma'am, dedicated Faculty Coordinators, invaluable Partners, and Sponsors for their unwavering support, which has made AMIMUN'24 possible. On behalf of the entire secretariat, I urge you to embrace this opportunity fully. Whether you are a seasoned delegate or new to the MUN world, rest assured that AMIMUN'24 will be a transformative experience.

Gauri Sareen,
Secretary General,
Amity International Model United Nations

GAURI SAREEN
